1 Prerequisites

For this R primer, you’ll need to read several chapters of Soetaert and Herman (2009) which are available on the Canvas website. Their book is an excellent source for those interested in ecosystem modeling. We will use bits of it along with this online text to get a little more comfortable with ecosystem processes and models.

References

Soetaert, K, and P M J Herman. 2009. A Practical Guide to Ecological Modelling. Springer.
